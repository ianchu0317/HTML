Personality

George is a loving little brother who looks up to and cares a lot for Peppa. He is timid and disobedient. He loves being given attention, and can become a little fussy at the drop of a hat. He is usually regarded as a crybaby, due to crying so much with his trademark fountain tears, but he's also shown to actually be a lot smarter then a child his age every now and then. Like any young boy, he has an interest in things like dinosaurs and space.


Appearance

George, being a toddler, is very small in comparison to most of the cast. He is pale pink with off-pink lips, has pink cheeks, and big wide eyes. He wears a blue shirt.
